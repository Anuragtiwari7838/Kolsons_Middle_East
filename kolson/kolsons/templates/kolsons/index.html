About EAO

EAO was founded in 1947 in the spirit of pioneers striving to turn to turn their innovative ideas into world famous products. EAO's first illuminated pushbuttons; a cornerstone of the companys future, combined design excellence and engineering expertise that set the standard for the entire industry.EAO has built on this reputation and flourished to become one of the world's leading businesses in the field of Human Machine Interface. EAO providing solutions across five continents and headquartered in Switzerland with factories in France, Germany & Switzerland.

About Kolsons Middle East


Established in the year 2016, "Kolsons Middle East FZC" is engaged in the process of importing, exporting and trading a technically advanced range of Electronic and Electrical Equipment. We are authorized distributor for EAO. Collaborating with companies having reputed products that are known for their high functionality and durability makes our product best in class. Our partners have manufacturing facilities located throughout Europe, namely Switzerland, Germany, France and Sweden and adhere to the strictest of quality norms. These product ranges are approved by al major certifying agencies and meet stringent quality norms. Kolsons Middle East FZC is catering to your prestigious requirement of various industrial and HMI products. Tecon Technologies has joined hands with EAO, Switzerland, Sweden for the supply of the products in India as well as the Middle East through its subsidiary Kolsons Middle East FZC to provide trusted and approved products for a variety of applications. Our aim is to render our best services to you in a timely and efficient manner and fulfill all your requirements.
